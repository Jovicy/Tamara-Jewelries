Return Policy

Every piece from Tamara & Afraser Jewelleries is crafted with care, precision, and attention to detail. Due to the nature of our products, all purchases are final sale.

This includes custom-made items, discounted pieces, and international orders. We do not accept returns, exchanges, or refunds once an order has been completed.

If you have any questions or concerns regarding your order, please contact us through our support channel. Our team will be happy to assist you.

Damaged Items

If your order arrives damaged, please contact us as soon as possible with the following details:

  • Your full name
  • Your order number
  • A brief description of the issue
  • Clear photos showing the damage

Once we review your request, we will work with you to resolve the issue appropriately.
