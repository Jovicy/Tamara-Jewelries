Privacy Policy

What information do we collect?

We collect information from you when you register on our site, place an order, subscribe to a newsletter, respond to a survey, or interact with other features on our website.

When ordering or registering, you may be asked to enter your name, email address, phone number, billing or shipping address, and payment details. You may also visit our site anonymously.

We may collect information about gift recipients solely for the purpose of fulfilling purchases. This information is not used for marketing purposes.

Like many websites, we use cookies to enhance your experience and gather information about visitors and visits.

How do we use your information?

We may use the information we collect in the following ways:

  • To personalize your experience and provide relevant content and products
  • To improve our website and customer service
  • To process transactions quickly and efficiently
  • To administer promotions, surveys, or other site features
  • To send periodic emails if you have opted in

If you no longer wish to receive promotional emails, you can opt out at any time.

How do we protect your information?

We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information. Your data is stored on secure networks and is only accessible by authorized personnel.

Sensitive information, such as payment details, is transmitted via Secure Socket Layer (SSL) technology and encrypted in our systems.

Do we use cookies?

Yes. Cookies are small files stored on your device that help us recognize your browser and remember certain information, such as items in your shopping cart and your preferences.

You can choose to disable cookies through your browser settings. However, some features of the site may not function properly if cookies are disabled.

Do we disclose your information to outside parties?

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your personally identifiable information to outside parties without your consent, except to trusted partners who assist us in operating our website and conducting our business, provided they agree to keep this information confidential.

We may release information when required to comply with the law, enforce our policies, or protect rights, property, or safety.

Non-personally identifiable information may be used for marketing, advertising, or analytics purposes.

Third-party links

Our website may include links to third-party websites. These sites have separate and independent privacy policies, and we are not responsible for their content or activities.

How can you update or delete your information?

You can update your personal information by accessing your account settings. To delete your account information, please contact us directly.

We may retain certain information for record-keeping and legal purposes.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page and will apply only to information collected after the update.

Questions and feedback

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our website.

Online Policy Only

This Privacy Policy applies only to information collected through our website and not to information collected offline.

Your Consent

By using our website, you consent to this Privacy Policy.
